Support:

Support for ZynAddSubFX can be obtained through a few different mediums. For general questions there is the public zynaddsubfx-user mailing list. To use the mailing list, first subscribe to the list and then send you email to zynaddsubfx-user AT lists dot sourceforge . net. Archives can also be found at the subscription link if you wish to see when a topic was last discussed.

For those who prefer forums, ZynAddSubFX has a sub-forum hosted at kvraudio . The kvraudio sub-forum tends to be an active hub for discussion of zyn on the windows platform.

Do you want a realtime alternative? ZynAddSubFX has a developer oriented IRC chat on Libera. Just pick your IRC client of choice, connect to irc.libera.chat, and join #zynaddsubfx.

If you run into a bug, please report it to the github tracker.
